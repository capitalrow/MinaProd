Features & Interactions

  • Mobile-First: Optimized for touch with 44px minimum touch targets
  • Keyboard Navigation: Use Arrow keys, Home, End, Enter, and Space
  • Smooth Scrolling: Snap points for aligned tab positioning
  • Scroll Indicators: Gradient fade edges show when more tabs are available
  • Active States: Clear visual feedback with indigo accent color
  • Accessibility: Full ARIA support and reduced motion preference
  • Responsive: Adapts from 320px phones to desktop screens
