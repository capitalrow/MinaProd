Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Mina. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our meeting transcription and insights platform.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access our platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Use our transcription services
  • Contact our support team
  • Subscribe to our newsletter
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This information may include:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, username, password
  • Profile Information: Job title, company name, preferences
  • Meeting Data: Audio recordings, transcriptions, meeting metadata
  • Communication Data: Messages, feedback, support requests

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our platform, we automatically collect certain information:

  • Usage Data: Features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system
  • Performance Data: Error logs, diagnostics, system performance metrics
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session data, preferences, analytics

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Provide transcription, insights, and platform features
  • Account Management: Create and manage your account, authenticate access
  • Improvements: Analyze usage to enhance our platform and user experience
  • Communication: Send notifications, updates, and support responses
  • Security: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • Legal Compliance: Meet legal obligations and enforce our terms
  • Marketing: Send promotional content (with your consent)

Data Security & Storage

Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security measures including AES-256 encryption for data at rest, TLS encryption for data in transit, bcrypt password hashing, and regular security audits.

Your data is stored securely on servers located in the United States. We maintain:

  • Encrypted databases with access controls
  • Regular automated backups
  • Intrusion detection and monitoring systems
  • Security incident response procedures

Data Retention: We retain your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party service providers to support our platform:

  • OpenAI: AI-powered transcription and insights generation
  • Replit: Platform hosting and infrastructure
  • Analytics Providers: Usage analytics and performance monitoring
  • Payment Processors: Secure payment processing (if applicable)

These providers have access only to the information necessary to perform their functions and are obligated to protect your data in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to certain data processing activities
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

GDPR Compliance: For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You have additional rights including the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this privacy policy and applicable data protection laws.

Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. We encourage you to review this privacy policy periodically.
